Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026 | Last Updated: May 29, 2026

1. Who We Are

Guinea Padre ("we," "us," or "our") is the author and publisher of children's picture books including The Sun in the Rain and A Fintastic Day at the Aquarium. Our website is guineapadre.shop.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate our services:

  • Contact Information: Your email address if you sign up for our newsletter or contact us.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregate, anonymous data about website visits and Pinterest engagement metrics via the Pinterest API. We do not collect personally identifiable Pinterest data from visitors.
  • Device Data: Browser type, operating system, and IP address (stored in anonymized server logs for security and debugging).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Communicate with you about books, blog updates, and promotions (only if you opt in).
  • Analyze anonymous Pinterest metrics to improve our content strategy and reach more families with SEL picture books.
  • Maintain website security and troubleshoot technical issues.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Pinterest: For OAuth authentication to access our own Pinterest analytics. We do not access or store other Pinterest users' data.
  • Service Providers: Hosting (GitHub Pages), email delivery (if newsletter is active), and analytics (anonymous, aggregate only).

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. We may use essential cookies for site functionality. Pinterest's API authentication follows OAuth 2.0 standards and does not involve cookie-based tracking of visitors.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above. Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely for trend analysis. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request information about how we process your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our books are for children ages 3–8, but our website and services are directed at parents, teachers, and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party retailers (Amazon) and social platforms (Pinterest, X). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
