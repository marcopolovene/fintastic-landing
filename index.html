The Story

When the Sky Has Other Plans

Laidee and Mills wake up buzzing with excitement, sunshine in their hearts and outdoor adventures dancing in their heads. But when the rain taps softly on the windowpane, everything feels like it might fall apart.

That's when Mommy pulls them close and shares a secret: sometimes what seems like a ruined day holds hidden gifts. With warmth, wonder, and the coziness of staying in, this is a story about learning to love what you have — even when it's raining.
