👋 Meet the Author

Guinea Padre

Hi! I'm Guinea Padre — a children's book author with one big mission: to inspire kids to love reading, explore the world around them, and feel the pure magic of a great story.

A Fintastic Day at the Aquarium was born from a love of wonder — the kind you see on a child's face the very first time they press their nose against aquarium glass and watch a shark glide silently past. I wanted to bottle that feeling and give it to every child, everywhere.

I hope this book brings as much joy to your family as it brought to me creating it. 🐠
