The Story

When the Sky Has Other Plans

Laidee and Mills wake up with hearts full of sunshine and a day of outdoor adventures ahead. But the rain arrives uninvited — and everything feels like it might fall apart. That's when Mommy does what she does best: she pulls her girls close and shares a secret that changes everything.

This is a story about finding beauty in unexpected moments, about the warmth of staying in when the world outside is wet, and about the kind of wisdom only a mother can give. Told through Guinea Padre's signature gentle storytelling and stunning illustrations, The Sun in the Rain is a book your family will reach for on every stormy afternoon.
