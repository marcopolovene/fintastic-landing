The Resilience Lessons in Guinea Padre Books

Both of Guinea Padre books are deeply rooted in emotional resilience. The Sun in the Rain models adaptability and finding unexpected joy. A Fintastic Day at the Aquarium teaches the serenity prayer - identifying what we can and cannot control, the foundation of all emotional resilience. Together they form a powerful SEL library for young children.
