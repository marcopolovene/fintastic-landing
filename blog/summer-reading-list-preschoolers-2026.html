7 Books Your Preschooler Will Love This Summer

Summer is the perfect time to build a reading habit that lasts a lifetime. These seven books are packed with adventure, emotion, and the kind of magic that makes a child ask for “one more page.”

    A Fintastic Day at the Aquarium — Guinea Padre

    Summer and aquariums go hand-in-hand. This ocean adventure is the perfect companion for a summer aquarium trip or a beach vacation. Real marine animals, a gentle lesson, and gorgeous illustrations. Get on Kindle

  • 3

    The Snowy Day by Ezra Jack Keats

    A timeless picture book about a child exploring the world outside on a magical day. The outdoor adventure spirit makes it a perennial summer read-aloud even in warm weather.

  • 4

    Chicka Chicka Boom Boom by Bill Martin Jr. & John Archambault

    Summer learning made joyful. This alphabet classic is upbeat, rhythmic, and impossible not to read aloud with energy. A perfect back-to-school bridge book.

  • 5

    If You Give a Mouse a Cookie by Laura Numeroff

    Chain-reaction storytelling that preschoolers adore. The circular plot structure helps develop cause-and-effect thinking — one of the key cognitive leaps of the preschool years.

  • 6

    Swimmy by Leo Lionni

    An underwater adventure about a little fish who uses creativity to solve a big problem. Summer, ocean, teamwork — it checks every box for warm-weather reading.

  • 7

    The Gruffalo by Julia Donaldson

    Brilliant rhymes, a clever protagonist, and just enough suspense to keep preschoolers riveted. A summer campfire read-aloud favorite for families everywhere.
