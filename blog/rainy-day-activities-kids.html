Activity Ideas

15 Rainy Day Activities Kids Actually Love

Creative

  • 1

    Rainy Day Watercolor Station

    Set up watercolor paints and let kids paint the rain outside the window, inspired by the golden tones of The Sun in the Rain. Display on the fridge when dry.

  • 2

    Blanket Fort Reading Den

    Build the ultimate cozy reading corner, grab every blanket and pillow, then settle in with The Sun in the Rain and a warm drink.

  • 3

    Story Illustration Project

    After reading the book, give kids paper and crayons to draw their own version of the story. A favorite early literacy activity for ages 3-7.

Learning

  • 4

    Rain in a Glass Experiment

    Fill a glass with water, add shaving cream on top, then drip food coloring and watch it rain through. Kids are absolutely mesmerized.

  • 5

    Window Nature Journal

    Observe and sketch rain, clouds, and puddles from inside. Talk about the water cycle. Connect it back to the book illustrations.

Play

  • 6

    Indoor Scavenger Hunt

    Hide objects around the house with rhyming clues. Race to find them all before the rain stops.

  • 7

    Laidee and Mills Puppet Show

    Use socks or paper bags to recreate scenes from The Sun in the Rain. Children become the author and love every minute.
